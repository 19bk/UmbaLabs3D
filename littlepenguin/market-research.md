## Key Market Segments
- **By age**: Kids 5-14 dominant; secondary market in vocational
- **By price**: Budget $20-35/month clubs → Premium ₦84,500/month online
- **By geography**: South Africa most developed; Nigeria/Kenya rapidly growing; Tanzania emerging
- **By delivery**: In-person classes, online platforms, school partnerships, holiday camps

## Market Drivers
- Google/UNESCO CogLabs partnership — 3D-printed education kits launched in Nigeria (Nov 2024)
- Kenyan-Chinese robotics partnership initiatives
- Growing government interest in STEM curriculum
- Youth unemployment driving demand for tech skills
- Class sizes typically 6-8 students (premium) or larger groups
