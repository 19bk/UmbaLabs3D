# LittlePenguin — Accelerators & Funding Opportunities

*Date: 2026-03-05*

## Why Not Base Batches
Base Batches Robotics Track (Virtuals Protocol) is focused on embodied AI for humanoid robots (Unitree G1) and is crypto/Web3 adjacent. LittlePenguin is an educational kit for 3-7 year olds — completely different domain. The facilities, mentorship, and network don't transfer.

---

## Application Strategy
1. **Build traction first** — 2-3 school pilots in Nairobi
2. **Apply with data** — "10 schools, 200 kids, 85% completion rate" beats any pitch deck
3. **Reference CogLabs** — Google/UNESCO already validated 3D-printed education kits in Africa
4. **Target UNICEF Innovation Fund** as first grant — no equity, perfect mission alignment
5. **HAX is the dream** — apply after prototype validation
