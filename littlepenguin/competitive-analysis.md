## Universal Weaknesses (Every Competitor)

### 1. Import-Dependent Hardware
Everyone uses LEGO, VEX, Arduino, micro:bit — all imported. "This item cannot be dispatched to your location."

### 2. Service Model
They sell classes/time, not a scalable product. Revenue stops when the teacher stops.

### 3. No Proprietary Kit
Anyone can copy their curriculum because they use off-the-shelf tools. Zero moat.

### 4. Expensive
$35/class, $350/course, ₦84,500/month. Prices out 90% of African families.

### 5. Age Gap
Nobody serves 3-5 year olds. Youngest is typically 5-6, most start at 8+.

## LittlePenguin's 5 Edges

### Edge 1: Own the Hardware
| | Them | LittlePenguin |
|---|---|---|
| Kit source | Imported (LEGO $200+, VEX $300+) | 3D printed locally, BOM ~KSh 5-7K |
| Lead time | 2-6 weeks shipping + customs | Print on demand, days |
| Customs/duty | 20%+ markup | Zero — locally made |
| Supply chain risk | "Cannot dispatch to your location" | Printer is the factory |
| Replacement parts | Order from abroad, wait weeks | Print same day |

### Edge 2: Price to Dominate
- LEGO Education Spike: KSh 45,000+
- VEX IQ Kit: KSh 39,000+
- Roboost 12 classes: KSh 45,000
- **LittlePenguin kit: KSh 8,500** (child keeps the robot, no recurring fees)

### Edge 3: Age 3-7 is Uncontested
| Competitor | Youngest Age |
|-----------|-------------|
| Cobot Kids | 5 (Scratch at 7) |
| Roboost | 5 |
| 9jacodekids | 6 |
| Ghana Robotics | 8+ |
| Khalmax | 8+ |

### Edge 4: Product vs Service
| | Service Model (Them) | Product Model (LittlePenguin) |
|---|---|---|
| Revenue | Stops when teacher stops | Kit sells while you sleep |
| Scale | Limited by instructors | Limited by printers |
| Margin | 30-50% (labor-heavy) | 60-70% (manufacturing) |
| Geography | Where you have teachers | Anywhere you can ship |
| Defensibility | Anyone can hire teachers | Patent the modular design |

### Edge 5: Razor and Blades
- Robot kit: KSh 8,500 (one-time)
- New scenario pack (field mat + 20 cards): KSh 1,500 each
- School curriculum pack (8 scenarios + teacher guide): KSh 15,000
- Seasonal/holiday themed packs: KSh 1,500 each
- **Recurring revenue from a hardware product**
