FAQ

Common Questions

What file formats do you accept?

Instant final quotes are available for STL files. Convert other formats (OBJ, STEP, 3MF) to STL before uploading.

How long does printing take?

Most orders are ready in 2-3 days. Larger or more complex prints may take longer. We'll give you an accurate timeline when we quote your project.

I don't have a 3D file. Can you help?

Yes! Send us a sketch, photo, or description of what you need. We offer design services to create 3D models from your ideas.

How do I pay?

We accept M-Pesa (preferred), bank transfer, and cash on pickup. Payment is required before we start printing.

Do you deliver?

Yes, we deliver within Nairobi. Pickup is also available. Delivery fees depend on your location.

What if I'm not happy with the print?

We do quality checks on every print. If there's a defect on our end, we'll reprint it for free. We also send photos before delivery so you can approve the result.

Is my design confidential?

Yes. We treat all customer files as confidential. We won't share, sell, or reuse your designs without permission.
