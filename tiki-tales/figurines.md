# Tiki Tales — Figurine Designs

## Design Specs
- **Height**: 60-80mm
- **Base**: flat circle, 40mm diameter, 3mm thick (houses NFC sticker in recessed pocket)
- **Style**: Rounded, chunky, toddler-safe (no sharp edges, no small parts)
- **Filament per figurine**: 15-25g
- **Print time**: 1-2 hours each
- **Cost**: KSh 50-80 (filament + NFC sticker)
- **Sell price**: KSh 500

## Launch Figurines (Phase 1 — 7 Characters)

### 1. Sungura (Hare)
- **Origin**: East African trickster tales (Kenya, Tanzania)
- **Stories**: "Sungura and the Well", "Hare Tricks Hyena", "The Clever Hare"
- **Design**: Standing hare with long ears, round body
- **Color**: White or natural PLA

### 2. Simba (Lion)
- **Origin**: Pan-African (king of the savanna)
- **Stories**: "Why Lion Roars", "The Lion's Share", "Simba and the Mouse"
- **Design**: Sitting lion with mane, proud posture
- **Color**: Gold/yellow PLA (Phase 2) or black (launch)

### 3. Kobe (Tortoise)
- **Origin**: Pan-African wisdom tales
- **Stories**: "Tortoise Wins the Race", "The Wise Tortoise", "Tortoise and the Birds"
- **Design**: Rounded shell, small head poking out
- **Color**: Green (Phase 2) or black (launch)

### 4. Anansi (Spider)
- **Origin**: West African (Akan/Ashanti, Ghana)
- **Stories**: "Anansi and the Pot of Wisdom", "How Anansi Got His Stories", "Anansi and the Moss-Covered Rock"
- **Design**: Round spider body with 4 visible legs (simplified, kid-safe)
- **Color**: Black

### 5. Nyamgondho (Fisherman)
- **Origin**: Luo folklore (Kenya, Lake Victoria)
- **Stories**: "Nyamgondho and the Magic Wife", traditional Luo tales
- **Design**: Simple human figure with fishing rod
- **Color**: Black or terracotta (Phase 2)

### 6. Mama (Sleepy / Bedtime) ⭐ NEW — Reddit-informed
- **Origin**: Universal
- **Content**: Lullabies, calm bedtime stories, white noise
- **Design**: Mother figure in restful pose, holding a baby or moon
- **Color**: White or natural PLA
- **Why V1**: Bedtime is the #1 use case per Reddit research. This is the gateway figurine — parents buy it first.

### 7. Creative Figurine (Record Your Own) ⭐ NEW — Promoted to V1
- **Content**: Blank — parent loads MP3s to a specific SD card folder
- **Design**: Simple abstract shape (star, heart, or blank character silhouette)
- **Color**: Any
- **How it works**: NFC UID maps to a folder on SD card. Parent copies MP3 files to that folder.
- **Why V1**: "Make your own content" is the #1 praised feature across all Reddit Yoto threads. Parents use it to:
  - Load audiobooks from their library
  - Record grandparent's voice reading stories (diaspora killer feature)
  - Create custom playlists
  - Load songs their kid loves without buying more figurines

## Print Considerations
- All figurines should be **printable without supports** (flat base, no overhangs >45°)
- **No small detachable parts** — everything solid, one piece
- Minimum wall thickness: 2mm
- NFC sticker pocket: 25mm diameter, 0.5mm deep recess in base
- Test: drop from 1m onto hard floor — must survive
