# 2.0.0 — Baseline toybox

Original lid and base, no RC522 features.

- `lid.stl` — identical to `../source/original-lid.stl`
- `base.stl` — identical to `../source/original-base.stl`
- Built from `../source/base.scad` (v5 speaker base, parametric)

**Dimensions:**
- Lid: 100 × 85 × 9.3 mm
- Base: 100 × 85 × 45 mm

**Print settings (Bambu A1):** PLA, 0.2 mm layers, 20 % gyroid, no supports, textured PEI plate, bed 55 °C.

**Known issues:** 4 obsolete mounting posts on lid interior (Ø4.5, at (23/77, 25.5/59.5)) — removed in 2.1.0.
